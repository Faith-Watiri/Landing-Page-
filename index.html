About Masterpiece Market

Masterpiece Market is an innovative platform where artists and creators showcase their work. Whether you're an artist looking to sell your creations or an art lover looking for unique pieces, this marketplace connects creativity with commerce, making art accessible to everyone.

Project Features

  • Secure login and user authentication
  • Personalized artist profiles
  • Search and filter artworks by category, price, and artist
  • Online payment system for purchasing art
  • User reviews and ratings for artworks

Technologies Used

  • Nestjs (Backend)
  • Flask (Web Framework)
  • React (Frontend)
  • PostgreSQL (Database)
  • Stripe (Payment Integration)
